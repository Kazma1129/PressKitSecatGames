Description

Help the Yellow Wizard overcome inner struggles on the finding of a cure to the Red Madness, in a platformer with unique card game mechanics. Overcome the challenges of the Madness, boost yourself with your deck of cards, look throw the red eyes and uncover the truth of the Underworld.

History

Red Madness was developed over the course of the last year by 2 students of XP University, Alvaro Sanchez (Mochagreen) and Mario Solorza (Kazma) as a thesis project.

Features

  • You are a wizard player: take control of the yellow wizard and explore the world in search of a cure for your family
  • Explore this fun and diverse 2D pixel world.
  • Test you skills by using different cards that would have you play a stage in different ways.
  • Skip the boring part of turn based games, and get active even when the enemy is in turn.
